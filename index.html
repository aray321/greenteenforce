About Green Teen Force

We're building a team of highly motivated teens dedicated to helping our community achieve sustainability goals and developing greater awareness about environmental issues.

Our Mission

To create a network of highly motivated teens with an eye for helping the broader community go green, bringing sustainability action to the forefront of the public's attention.

We identify key environmental research opportunities undertaken by various agencies and non-profits and enable students to apply, match their skills and perform this research work. Our goal is to give passionate students the opportunity to engage in meaningful work in their community.

Our Purpose

To help connect motivated youth, regardless of background, to research and volunteer opportunities that make an impact on the fight for sustainability, and to bring these skills together to make tangible impacts in advancing sustainable goals within the community.

We hope to reduce the knowledge barrier for going green within our community, and drive greater awareness for the benefits of going green in both households and small businesses.
